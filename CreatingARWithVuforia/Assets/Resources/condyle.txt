Condyle

Fact
The condyle is rounded protuberance at the end of some bones, forming an articulation with the upper jaw of the hippo. It is an important feature of the bones to note. The Hippos mandibular condyle is placed in such a way that the upper mandible can house the eyes, ears, and nostrils high on the skull to support breathing every 3-5 minutes while the body is submerged in water
